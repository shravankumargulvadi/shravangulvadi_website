Overview:In this project, I tried to condense the vast narrative of the universe's creation into a one-minute video, premiered anonymously at a demoscene event and was voted as one of the top 5 videos. "A Journey through the Cosmos" takes viewers from the void before the Big Bang to humanity's first forays into space, capturing the awe-inspiring journey with vivid but simple animations. This piece merges technical prowess with artistic vision, rendering a visual poem about the beginnings of time and our quest to understand it. It's a digital homage to the curiosity that drives us to explore beyond our limits.
