Introduction

Welcome! I'm an engineer with a rich background in robotics and autonomy, holding a Master's degree from Carnegie Mellon University. I am interested in making robots work under uncertainty and clutter of real world environments and in designing the necessary robotic software and hardware to achieve this. Currently, I apply my expertise as a Controls Engineer at Mainspring Energy. My professional journey includes a stint as a Robotics Research Intern at Voaige and over four years as a Product Development Engineer in India's defense sector. My website is a window into my experiences and ongoing endeavors in the ever-evolving world of engineering.
